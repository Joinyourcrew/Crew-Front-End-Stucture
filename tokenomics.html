CREW Tokenomics Whitepaper
Executive Summary
The CREW token powers a dynamic ecosystem designed to foster decentralized innovation, reward community engagement, and support the launch of partner projects. With a carefully structured tokenomics model, CREW balances growth, sustainability, and value creation through strategic allocations, vesting schedules, and deflationary mechanisms. Key features include a team-aligned purchase model, a robust treasury for ecosystem investments, and a buyback-and-burn program to enhance token scarcity. This whitepaper outlines the CREW tokenomics, emphasizing transparency, long-term incentives, and investor confidence.

1. Token Overview
Token Name: CREW
Total Supply: [To be specified by the project, e.g., 1,000,000,000 CREW]
Purpose: Drive ecosystem participation, fund partner token launches, and incentivize community engagement.
Utility:
Governance: Vote on treasury disbursements and platform upgrades.
Staking: Earn rewards for locking CREW tokens, supporting network stability.
Premium Access: Unlock exclusive features, such as early access to partner token sales.
Partner Token Access: Use CREW to participate in launches hosted on the platform.
2. Initial Allocation and Team Purchase
The CREW team will purchase 10% of the total token supply at launch to align incentives with the community. This allocation is subject to:

Lockup Period: 100% locked for 3 months post-launch to demonstrate long-term commitment.
Vesting Schedule: Post-lockup, 0.5% of the team’s allocation (5% of the 10%) unlocks biweekly over approximately 20 months.
Allocation of Unlocked Tokens:
50% to CREW Treasury: Funds investments in partner token launches and ecosystem liquidity.
25% to Top Users/Holders: Rewards for active participants and long-term holders, distributed via staking or engagement metrics.
25% to Operational Costs: Supports platform development, marketing, and partnerships.
3. CREW Matching Mechanism
To reward the team for a successful launch, CREW will match the team’s purchase up to 5% of the total supply. This allocation is subject to:

Lockup Period: 100% locked for 2 months post-launch.
Vesting Schedule: Post-lockup, tokens vest linearly over 4 weeks (1.25% of the matched allocation per week).
Purpose: Incentivizes team performance while ensuring alignment with ecosystem goals.
